Newtown Discovery Walk with Shawn S.

– Newtown

Hello, or Nihao, my name is Shawn, a man with keen eyes for everything different. Originally a kid from Taiwan, I have learnt, lived and breathed like an Australian for the past ten years.

Take a two-hour adventure with me through the cool part of Sydney, the inner hub of Newtown to live and experience like a true Sydney-sider! Shop that only sells buttons? Check! Music and band you have never heard of? Check! Awesome coffee? Check! Amazing cakes?! Check! By the end of the adventure you will see Newtown through the eyes of a Sydney local.

Please wear comfortable clothing and be aware that there may be dairy products involved.

Japanese Bars and Izakaya with Jun K.

– Sydney CBD

I am a Japanese born long term resident of Sydney. WIth annual trips back to Tokyo I'm in touch with the latest from my home country. I've lived and partied around inner city Sydney for 7 years and know all the Japanese places.

There's an exploding Japanese bar and Izakaya scene in Sydney. The days of sushi trains or nothing are well behind us! I've picked three great places to visit:

- A tiny hidden bar for a drink and Japanese snacks

- A yakatori specialist for a second drink

- An innovative Izakaya for a modern take on sushi and plenty of alternative main meals OR a fiery Japanese BBQ if you prefer.

The experience fee includes 2 drinks and 2 snacks (one each at the first two places) ; allow an additional $15-$30 per person for final restaurant meals according to your choice.
